FAQ

CALT focuses on a highly visual, intuitive "scrolling" interface to make discovering events fast and enjoyable. Our AI-driven personalization ensures you see what's most relevant to you, cutting through the noise of traditional event listings.

We're solving "event fatigue" and the fear of missing out (FOMO). It's hard to keep track of everything happening. CALT simplifies discovery, making it easy and fun to find cultural events you'll genuinely love.

Our core values are centered on community, culture, and connection. We aim to support local organizers, make cultural events more accessible, and help people discover new experiences that enrich their lives.

CALT is for culturally curious individuals, whether you're a local resident of Athens or a visitor. It's for anyone who wants to spend less time searching and more time experiencing the vibrant cultural scene.
